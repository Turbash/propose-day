## 📝 Customize It!

### Quick Start:

1. Open: `propose-day-website/src/config.ts`
2. Change:
   - `personName` - Your loved one's name
   - `openingQuote` - Your romantic quote
   - All page messages
   - Photo URLs
3. Save and watch it update!

### Add Your Photos:

**Option 1: Your PPT Slides** (Already Done!)
- Location: `public/slides/1.jpg` to `10.jpg` ✅
- Used in Page 9 carousel

**Option 2: Memory Photos**
- Edit `photos` array in config.ts
- Add 6 photo URLs
- Add captions
